# Dayflow HRMS - IT Support & Security

## 1. Requesting IT Support
- **Ticket System**: For any hardware or software issues, please submit a ticket via the IT Helpdesk Portal.
- **Emergency Support**: For critical issues such as system outages, contact the internal IT escalation channel or HR admin.
- **Standard Response Time**: 4 hours for high priority, 24 hours for normal priority.

## 2. Security Best Practices
- **Passwords**: Must be at least 12 characters and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)**: Mandatory for all company applications (Slack, Email, HR Portal, VPN).
- **Phishing**: Be vigilant! Report any suspicious emails using the "Report Phishing" button in your email client.

## 3. Remote Access & VPN
- Use the company-provided VPN whenever accessing internal resources from a public or home network.
- Do not store sensitive company data on personal devices.
- Attendance check-ins from unapproved networks may be logged but flagged for HR review.

## 4. Hardware Policy
- Laptops are replaced every 3 years.
- Lost or stolen equipment must be reported to IT and Security immediately.
- Use of unauthorized software or peripherals is strictly prohibited for security reasons.

## 5. Software Updates
- Critical security updates will be pushed automatically and may require a system restart. Please save your work when prompted.
