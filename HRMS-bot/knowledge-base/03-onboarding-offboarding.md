# Dayflow HRMS - Onboarding & Offboarding

## 1. Onboarding Process
Welcome strictly follows our established path to get you integrated swiftly and securely.
- **Pre-Arrival**: Background checks, signed offer letter collection, IT equipment provisioning.
- **Day 1**: Orientation session, IT systems login, review of the employee handbook.
- **Week 1**: Departmental introductions, role-specific training, meeting with your assigned buddy.
- **Month 1**: 30-day check-in with your manager and HR to ensure a smooth transition.

## 2. IT and Accounts Setup
- **Email & Slack**: Provided on Day 1.
- **HRMS Portal Access**: Login details will be sent via personal email. Here you can view payslips, manage leave, update personal info, check attendance, and manage assigned tasks.
- **Dayflow-Bot Access**: Employees can use Dayflow-Bot from their registered WhatsApp number. The number must match the employee profile phone number in E.164 format.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Mandatory for all company accounts.

## 3. Offboarding Process
When an employee resigns or their employment is terminated, the offboarding process ensures a smooth transition.
- **Notice Period**: Standard notice period is 2 weeks (checking employment contract for exceptions).
- **Exit Interview**: An optional, confidential interview with HR to provide feedback.
- **Knowledge Transfer**: The departing employee must document their current projects and hand over responsibilities.
- **Equipment Return**: All company equipment (laptop, badge, keys) must be returned on the final day.
- **Final Pay**: The final paycheck, including any accrued unpaid PTO, will be processed on the next regular payroll cycle.
